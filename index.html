On July 30, 1993, Future Crew debuted Second Reality at Assembly 93. In April 2022 I realized that the 30th anniversary was coming up, so I thought that someone should make an HTML5 remake.

I started one, but I don't have any experience coding demos so I will probably be unable to finish it in time. Therefore, I'm releasing what I got after one month of work in the hope that someone else can take the idea and run with it.

  • Based on Second Reality by Future Crew: Marvel, Pixel, Purple Motion, Skaven, Psi, Trug, Wildfire, Abyss, Gore.
  • Code by me.
  • The music is the original S3M, played in real time.
  • The planet landscape was redrawn based on the original pixel art.
  • The ship models were extracted from the original demo.
  • Uses three.js for the 3D scenes.
  • The font is "SR serif", based on Merriweather.
